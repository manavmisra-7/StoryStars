About Us

We are Arnav, Manav, Shivam, Pranay Aarush & Arav. A group of enthusiastic school going kids who want to express themselves .We love to dream, to imagine and weave our ideas into a beautiful portrait which will appeal to most imaginative kids. We love our stories, our little butterflies and we want them to soar higher in times to come. We come from different backgrounds and form one family called "Story Stars".
